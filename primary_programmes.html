Guided Nature Walks

In this workshop, children will learn about:

- Local Biodiversity in Singapore
- Basic plant and animal identification
- The sounds and textures of nature
- How to respect and protect natural spaces

Please note:

While our biodiversity guided walks aim to explore the rich ecosystems and diverse wildlife in natural habitats, sightings of specific animals are not guaranteed. Wildlife visibility depends on various factors, including weather, time of day, and animal behavior. Our focus is to provide an immersive and educational experience, highlighting the importance of conserving natural habitats and understanding biodiversity as a whole. We appreciate your understanding that the unpredictability of nature is part of its beauty, and we look forward to sharing valuable insights into these ecosystems with you.



Beach / Urban Cleanups

In this workshop, children will learn about:

- Local Biodiversity in Singapore and its connection to clean habitats
- The impact of litter on marine and urban wildlife
- Ways to reduce waste and recycle effectively

Activity highlights include:

- Hands-on cleanup of a beach or urban area
- Sorting collected litter for recycling vs. waste
- Fun team-based activities to build a sense of community and responsibility



Plastic Recycling Workshop

In this workshop, children will learn about:

- The importance of recycling to protect local ecosystems and wildlife
- How plastics impact local biodiversity and the food chain
- Simple ways to reduce plastic use in everyday life

Activity highlights include:

- Hands-on activities with recyclable materials, allowing children to safely explore the recycling process
- Creative repurposing projects where children can transform plastic items into useful or decorative objects
- Interactive discussions about the environmental impact of plastic and the importance of mindful recycling practices



Urban Tiller

In this workshop, children will learn about:

- The basics of urban gardening and the importance of green spaces in cities
- The role of plants in supporting local biodiversity and reducing urban heat
- How to care for plants, including watering, soil types, and sunlight needs

Activity highlights include:

- Hands-on planting experience where children can pot their own plants to take home
- Ideation games, fostering creative thinking around farming challenges



Biodiversity Workshops

In this workshop, children will learn about:

- The importance of wildlife and their role in our ecosystems
- How humans and wildlife can live harmoniously in urban areas
- Responsible actions to support local wildlife and reduce human-wildlife conflicts
- How different species interact within ecosystems
- The importance of biodiversity conservation and how we can help protect natural habitats

Activity highlights include:

- Interactive sessions with animal plushies and specimen models to introduce different species
- Hands-on activities simulating ecosystems and food chains
- Role-playing activities to understand biodiversity behavior and needs
