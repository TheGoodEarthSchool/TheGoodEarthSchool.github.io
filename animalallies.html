Animal Allies (Suitable for ages 4-16)

Understanding animals and what you can do to care for them!

Suitable for ages 4–18, organized into the following groups:

  • Kindergarten Group (K1 & K2): Ages 4–6
  • Lower Primary Group (Primary 1 to 3): Ages 7–9
  • Upper Primary Group (Primary 4 to 6): Ages 10–12
  • Secondary Group (Secondary 1 to 4/5): Ages 13–16

Workshops in this series

  • Friends of Dogs
  • Friends of Cats
  • Friends of Guinea Pigs
  • Friends of Rabbits
  • Friends of Terapins
  • Friends of Quails

Friends of Dogs

In this workshop, children will learn about:

- Common Misconceptions of the animal
- Dietary Requirements
- Housing
- Basic Animal Behaviour and Care

Activity highlights include:

- Animal interaction*
- Hands-on with real pet care items
- Fun team-based activities to build a sense of community and responsibility

*In the interest of both participant safety and animal welfare, live animals may or may not be used during the workshops. Unfamiliar environments can sometimes be stressful for animals + literature has shown that alternative methods like interactions with plushies or animal models can achieve similar learning outcomes. When live animals are part of the session, the activity will always be supervised by a seasoned animal handler to ensure the well-being and the safety of all participants and animals. However, do kindly note that the amount of direct interaction with live animals are not guaranteed.
